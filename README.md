# AtliQ Hardware — Sales Analytics Reports

## 📌 Project Overview

This project contains sales performance reports for **AtliQ Hardware**, analyzing customer and market-level net sales data across fiscal years 2019, 2020, and 2021. The reports help identify top-performing customers, high-growth markets, and gaps against sales targets.

---

## 📂 Files Included

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `customer_performance_report.pdf` | Net sales breakdown by customer for FY2019–2021 with YoY growth % |
| `market_performance_vs_target.pdf` | Country-level net sales vs. 2021 targets with variance analysis |

---

## 📊 Report 1: Customer Performance Report

**Purpose:** Tracks net sales per customer across three fiscal years and measures 2021 vs 2020 growth.

### Key Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Customer | Name of the retail/e-commerce customer |
| Net Sales | Cumulative net sales (all years combined) |
| NetSales 19 | Net sales in FY2019 |
| NetSales 20 | Net sales in FY2020 |
| NetSales 21 | Net sales in FY2021 |
| 2021 vs 2020 | % growth of FY2021 over FY2020 |

### Highlights

- **Grand Total Net Sales:** $883.0M across all years
- **FY2021 Total:** $598.9M (vs $196.7M in FY2020) — **304.48% growth**
- **Top 3 Customers by Net Sales:**
  - Amazon — $131.8M
  - AtliQ Exclusive — $88.4M
  - Atliq e Store — $83.8M
- **Highest Growth Customers (2021 vs 2020):**
  - Nova — 2664.92%
  - Integration Stores — 887.19%
  - Chiptec — 722.03%

---

## 🌍 Report 2: Market Performance vs Target

**Purpose:** Compares actual FY2021 net sales against targets for each country/market and shows variance.

### Key Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Customer | Country/market name |
| Net Sales | Cumulative net sales (all years) |
| NetSales 19 | Net sales in FY2019 |
| NetSales 20 | Net sales in FY2020 |
| NetSales 21 | Actual net sales in FY2021 |
| Actual - Target | Absolute gap vs 2021 target (all negative = missed target) |
| % | % variance from target |

### Highlights

- **Grand Total Variance:** -$54.9M (-8%) — all markets missed their 2021 targets
- **Largest Market:** India — $241.9M net sales, -$9.6M vs target (-6%)
- **Biggest Miss (Absolute):** USA — -$10.2M (-10%)
- **Biggest Miss (%):** Poland — -15%
- **Closest to Target:** Japan & Portugal — only -4% off

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

1. **Massive YoY Growth** — FY2021 saw 3x growth over FY2020, showing strong post-pandemic recovery or business expansion.
2. **All Markets Missed Targets** — Despite strong growth, targets were set too aggressively; every single country fell short.
3. **India is the Largest Market** — Contributes ~27% of total net sales.
4. **E-commerce Dominance** — Amazon ($131.8M) and Atliq e Store ($83.8M) are among the top performers, reflecting a digital sales shift.
5. **Small Customers Growing Fast** — Nova (2664%), Integration Stores (887%), Chiptec (722%) show high growth from a small base.
